Contact and Information

The Foolish Fish Collection is a unique NFT art collection first released in late 2021. We follow the ERC-721 standard. There is a fixed 30 piece collection available. No more will be minted for series 1, making them incredibly rare. In early to mid 2022 look for the second series of the collection to be released. Feel free to leave a message to receive more information on the collection, the artist or general questions. You can also reach out directly to any of the social media accounts provided.
